About


As a kid, I loved to stay up for hours creating small, online video games. My natural curiosity drove me to research algorithms, experiment with different game genres, or learn new drawing techniques. More than just exploring new things, it motivated me to improve and perfect my work by actively seeking out problems that needed solving.

I continued these passions for design and technology into college, where I worked part-time as an Educational Technologist, building community by teaching others how to do what I do. As a liberal arts student, I learned to adapt my skills to almost any field I found myself in.

In my free time, I’m often found cooking a new recipe, designing a game, or drawing with my cat on my lap! ₍^. .^₎⟆
